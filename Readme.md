# Quantitative Study of Conditions of Civil War Recovery Based on International Intervention:
## Introduction
This is the hub for my continuing work on researching the requirements for success of civil war recovery in different kinds of civil wars -- most notably, between those that feature a heavy amount of external involvement and multiparty wars compared to more traditional ones. Inspired by the ongoing Sudanese civil war and the semi-concluded Syrian civil war (at least tentatively) and [Sharif's "Predicting the End of the Syrian Conflict" (2019)](https://doi.org/10.1080/1057610X.2018.1538153), this studies whether wars of different substance have different requirements for reconstruction.

## Progress:
This is the first week of actual work on this -- currently, just some data aggregation and preliminary research (especially on definitions) have been done. For an overview, see the [first week's report](./updates/week1/week1.pdf).
